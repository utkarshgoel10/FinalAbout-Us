Who We Are

Founded in 2021 by Utkarsh Goel & Rohit Chaudhary, Careerhub has come a long way from its beginnings in Ghaziabad. When we first started, our passion for making an easy-to-operate career guide drove us to find some time from our college studies so that Careerhub can offer you the world’s best career advice or the world’s best growth platform. We now serve customers all over India and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our website. We hope you enjoy our products & services as much as we enjoy offering them to you.
